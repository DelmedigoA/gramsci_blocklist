Gramsci Blocklist

Gramsci Blocklist is a Google Sheets™ add-on that helps identify and manage blacklisted phone numbers directly within spreadsheets.

Home Privacy Terms Support

What it does

  • Scans the active sheet for phone numbers.
  • Checks numbers against a blocklist retrieved via an external API.
  • Highlights and provides actions (for example: mark/remove rows) via a dedicated menu in Google Sheets™.
